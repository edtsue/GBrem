Rembrand places the Googlebook in the moment — on the desk, in the room — never in an actor's hands. The Glowbar lid light does the identifying: audiences clock a Googlebook at a glance, no "as-used" shot required.

Magic Pointer

Built with DeepMind — wiggle the cursor and Gemini comes alive, offering contextual suggestions for whatever you point at. Ask, compare and combine.

Create your Widget

Prompt a custom dashboard; Gemini pulls from the web, Gmail and Calendar to assemble it — flights, hotels and a trip countdown in one place.

Magic Cue

Ported from Pixel — surfaces the next action from the context of your messages and email, right on the laptop.

Phone app streaming

Stream any Android app from a nearby phone into a floating window — order food or finish a Duolingo lesson without leaving your screen.

Quick Access

View, search and insert your phone's files straight from the Googlebook file browser — no transfers.
